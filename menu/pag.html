The Cube

  • More Difficulty?

  • 2 players

Objectives

You've to pick the coins(cubes green) without touching the enemies(cubes yellows).

You move with the arrows and if you select the 2 players mode, with keys WASD.

In addition to pause the game with the button in the top right, you can also with the space key.

In the Challenge Mode, you can't play with 2 players(At the moment).
